Top Outreach Strategies for 2025

By Sarah Khan, RankLinkus

Outreach is about people, not bulk emails. In 2025, personalization and relevance are essential. Publishers get fewer low quality pitches, so a concise, value led outreach message wins.

Practical outreach tactics

  • Research author interests, recent articles, and suggest a relevant angle
  • Offer exclusive content, or data that helps the author write an even better piece
  • Follow up twice, space messages by 5 to 10 days, be polite and concise
  • Use a CRM or spreadsheet to track replies, pitch status, and next steps

Outreach tools

Tools for prospecting, email warmup, and tracking can help, but the craft is in the message and the relationship. Combine software with human review for best results.
